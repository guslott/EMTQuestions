EMT Pharmacology Study Questions

  1. What is the primary effect of administering nitroglycerin?
    • A) Increases heart rate
    • B) Relaxes muscle walls of coronary arteries
    • C) Constricts muscle walls of coronary arteries

  2. What medication form is administered through the rectum?
    • A) Tablets
    • B) Capsules
    • C) Solutions

    D) Suppositories

    • A) Faster absorption through the digestive system
    • B) Avoidance of the first-pass metabolism
    • C) Slower release into the bloodstream

    B) Avoidance of the first-pass metabolism
  4. Which of the following is NOT a route for administering medication?
    • A) Intravenous

    • C) Pharmacodynamics

    A) Adverse effects
  6. What does the 'Right patient' of the 'Rights of medication administration' refer to?
    • A) Giving the medication to the correct patient

    • C) Administering the right dose to the patient
    • D) Assessing the patient before giving medication
    A) Giving the medication to the correct patient
  7. What is the effect of a medication classified as an 'antagonist'?
    • A) It stimulates receptors to produce an effect

    B) It blocks receptors to prevent other chemicals from binding
